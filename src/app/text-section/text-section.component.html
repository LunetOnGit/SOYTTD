What is this all about?

Simulation of Your Turn to Die is a game in the same genre as Hunger Games Simulator. However, this time you’ll get to generate a Your Turn to Die death game scenario, either from the actual characters, or with anyone else since you’ll get to upload your own images, change every character with their abilities.

This game is basically a remake of the one on Itch (also made by me), I tried to keep the same as before, I hope you’ll enjoy!


Custom Games :o

If you’ve played Simulation of Your Turn to Die on itch (made by me), then you should know that the death games are “semi-random”, which means that the characters will randomly be put in some situations, but they’ll (almost) always act the same way, this is due to statistics I gave them.

Good news is, that you’ll get the opportunity to custom a lot of stuff!

You’ll get to choose the number of participants, choose their stats (strength, speed, qi, kindness...) but also their relationship with everyone (I’m personally using it for the Yabusames, along with Joe/Sara, Mishima/Nao, Keiji/Megumi...)
